The Hobbit, or There and Back Again

J.R.R Tolkien

Book Description

"The Hobbit, or There and Back Again" by J.R.R. Tolkien is a fantasy adventure that follows Bilbo Baggins, a reluctant Hobbit, who joins 13 Dwarves and the wizard Gandalf on a journey to reclaim a stolen treasure from the dragon Smaug. Along the way, Bilbo encounters trolls, goblins, spiders, and more, discovering his bravery and finding the mysterious One Ring, which ties into the events of "The Lord of the Rings."

Genre:Fantasy, Adventure
Pages:310
Published:September 21, 1937
